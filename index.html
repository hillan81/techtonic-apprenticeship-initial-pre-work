Marvin Dolo

Musical Artist With an Immense Stylistic Range.

About The Artist

Born and raised in Brooklyn NY, Marvin Dolo has been releasing music since 2017. Through eight project releases, he has bounced back and forth between the most experimental walls of hip-hop and his own uniquely enigmatic brand of R&B. One project may demonstrate Dolo’s more melodically inclined romantic side whereas another will exemplify his contribution to the bass-thumping hip-hop wave. Marvin Dolo has proved himself to be spectacular in his creative bipolarism and if you’re a fan of hip-hop, R&B, and what the two scenes are becoming at the hands of the world’s most experimental and wide-ranging musicians, you’ll love each of his 8 projects.

Why I Like Marvin Dolo

Music is a big part of my everyday life. I love how music can be used to bring people together, help you get over hardships, relax you, energize you and so much more. Marvin Dolo is one of those musicians who contains a catalog of songs for any mood and that’s the main reason he’s one of my favorites. The five main reasons why Marvin Dolo is one of my favorite artists are:

  • The relatability that his music conveys.
  • The versatility both with his melodies and subject matter.
  • The positivity and reality that he conveys in his projects.
  • His catalog spanning 239 minutes of genre-defying vibes for any mood.
  • He merges my two favorite music genres: R&B and Hip-Hop.
